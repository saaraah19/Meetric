What it does

Everything a sales rep does.
None of the delays.

🌐

Detects language instantly

The first message determines the language — Arabic, French, or English — and the entire conversation stays in that language. No settings, no language selection screen.

🎯

Structured qualification

Asks four questions every salesperson needs: what they need, their budget, their timeline, and how to reach them. Adapts follow-ups based on what's already been said.

🔥

Hot / Warm / Cold scoring

Transparent, code-based rules — not an AI self-report. Budget ≥ $1,000 and timeline ≤ 4 weeks = hot. The thresholds are tunable per client in five seconds.

⚡

Instant Slack alerts

Hot leads trigger an immediate Slack notification with the full profile. Every lead, regardless of score, is logged to Notion as a permanent CRM record.

📅

Google Calendar booking

Hot and warm leads are offered real available slots pulled live from Google Calendar. Confirming a slot creates the event, sends the invite to the lead's email, and blocks the time — automatically.

📤

CSV export

Every lead is exportable as a structured CSV — contact info, need, budget, timeline, score, timestamp. Drops straight into any CRM or spreadsheet.

The flow

A conversation with a purpose
at every stage.

01

Greet

Detects the visitor's language from their first message and opens in kind.

02

Qualify

Collects need, budget, timeline, and contact — one at a time, conversationally.

03

Score

Scores the lead hot/warm/cold using explicit rules. Sends a Slack alert if hot.

04

Book

Queries Google Calendar in real time, offers the next free slots, and creates the event with a calendar invite the moment the lead confirms.
